Ingredients:
 - 2 ripe Hass avocados
 - 2 tps lime juice
 - 2 Tbl cilantro (chopped)
 - 1/4c red onion (finely chopped)
 - 1/4c tsp kosher salt

Directions:
 1. Cut avocados in half and remove the pits.  Scoop out the avocados and place
    in a medium bowl.
 
 2. Toss and coat with lime juice.  Add the salt and using a fork or potato
    masher, mash until a smooth consistency is achieved.

 3. Fold in the remaining ingredients and mix well.

 4. Taste the guacomole and adjust seasoning as needed.
